Monster Death:
Monster Death is a single player 2D platform game. 
Player need to kill monsters by shooting the fireballs.
Player should collect gems and in the end of the level player needs to collect the potion and can move into the next level.

Steps to develop game.
1.Environment Design.
2.Player movement and jump.
3.Fireball spawning.
4.Gems collection.
5.updating score.
6.Destroy monsters- if fireball collides with monster should destroy.
7. If player collides with monster Gameover text should be displayed. 
8.if player reaches to the potion and collects it next level should be loaded and Game Won text should be displayed.
 
Win Condition: When player reaches end of the level and all monsters should be destroyed.
Loose Condition: If player collides with monster then Game ends.
